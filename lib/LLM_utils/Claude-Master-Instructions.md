## Resume Structure & Length Requirements

### Content Volume

- **Minimum:** 2 full pages (experienced professionals)
- **Target:** 2.5–3 pages (senior roles, 7+ years experience)
- **Professional Summary:** 4–5 lines with keyword density
- **Technical Skills:** 25–35 skills across 4–5 categories
- **Work Experience:** 4–6 thematic subsections per role
- **Each bullet:** 2–3.5 lines using STAR method with metrics

### Work Experience Architecture

Use this exact structure for every role:

```
[Job Title]
[Company] | [Dates]

[Theme 1 — Primary Job Requirements]
- [STAR bullet: Situation/Task + Action with technical specifics + Result with 2–3 metrics + timeframe]
- [STAR bullet with comprehensive technical details and quantified business impact]

[Theme 2 — Technical Excellence & Innovation]
- [Challenge context + Technical solution implemented + Performance/efficiency gains with % + adoption/scale metrics]

[Theme 3 — Leadership & Cross-functional Impact]
- [Team/project context + Leadership actions + Team performance improvements + business outcomes]

[Theme 4 — Process Optimization & Strategic Impact]
- [Process challenge + Optimization strategy + Implementation approach + Cost/time/quality improvements]
```

Add Theme 5 and Theme 6 subsections as needed for senior roles or roles with significant scope.

---

## STAR Method — Mandatory for Every Bullet

Every achievement bullet must follow this structure:

- **Situation/Task** (15–20 words): Brief context or challenge
- **Action** (25–35 words): Specific actions taken with technical depth, using a verb from Action-Verbs
- **Result** (15–25 words): Quantified outcomes with 2–3 specific metrics
- **Business Impact + Timeframe** (10–15 words): Strategic value and duration

**Target total per bullet:** 65–95 words for comprehensive coverage

---

## Action Verb Protocol

For every bullet point written:

- Consult Action-Verbs before selecting a verb
- Select the verb that best matches the achievement type and seniority level
- Vary verbs throughout — never repeat the same verb across bullets in the same role
- Match verb strength to the significance of the achievement
- Maintain consistent past tense for previous roles, present tense for current role

## ATS Optimization Standards

Apply all rules from ATS-Cheatsheet. Key requirements:

- File format: .docx for submission; LaTeX source for final delivery
- Single-column layout, standard fonts only
- Keyword density: 2–4% for primary keywords; 85%+ overall relevance score
- ATS parsing compatibility: 95%+ required
- No tables, graphics, headers/footers, or special characters
- Standard section headers only (exact terminology per ATS-Cheatsheet)
- Primary keywords appear 3–5 times; secondary keywords 2–3 times
- Acronyms spelled out on first use, then abbreviated

---

## Technical Accuracy Requirements

Every resume must include:

- All technologies, versions, and frameworks explicitly named
- Scale quantification: users served, data volumes processed, systems managed
- Business context: revenue impact, cost savings, efficiency gains
- Timeline specificity: exact durations and deadlines met
- Collaboration scope: cross-functional team size and stakeholder involvement
